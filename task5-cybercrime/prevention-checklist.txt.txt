============================================================
           STAY SAFE ONLINE - PREVENTION CHECKLIST          
============================================================

[1] VERIFICATION OF LINKS
    - Never click on suspicious links via WhatsApp or SMS.
    - Always check if the URL starts with "https://".

[2] UPI PIN SAFETY (FINANCIAL)
    - IMPORTANT: UPI PIN is ONLY for sending money.
    - If someone says "enter PIN to receive money," it's a SCAM.

[3] UPI ID & QR CODES (FINANCIAL)
    - Do not scan random QR codes sent by strangers.
    - Scammers use fake codes to authorize illegal payments.

[4] TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA)
    - Enable 2FA on Gmail, Instagram, and LinkedIn.
    - Adds an extra layer of security beyond your password.

[5] AVOID PUBLIC WI-FI
    - Don't do banking on free Wi-Fi (Airports/Cafes).
    - Hackers can easily intercept your data on open networks.

[6] APP PERMISSIONS
    - Always review what an app wants to access.
    - A simple app should not need your Gallery or Contacts.

[7] SOFTWARE UPDATES
    - Keep your phone OS and Apps updated regularly.
    - Updates contain critical "Security Patches".

[8] OFFICIAL REPORTING CHANNELS (IMMEDIATE ACTION)
    - National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
    - National Helpline Number  : 1930
