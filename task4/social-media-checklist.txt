# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

### **Do's (Recommended Practices)**
* **Maintain Privacy:** Regularly check your privacy settings to ensure your personal information is only visible to trusted individuals.
* **Think Before Posting:** Always consider if your post reflects a professional image, as future employers often check social media.
* **Verify Information:** Fact-check any news or data before sharing to prevent the spread of misinformation.
* **Network Professionally:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to connect with industry experts and showcase your academic achievements.
* **Respect Others:** Be polite in comments and discussions, even when you disagree with someone’s opinion.

### **Don'ts (Practices to Avoid)**
* **Avoid Oversharing:** Never post sensitive details like your home address, phone number, or official ID cards.
* **No Cyberbullying:** Avoid using offensive language, trolling, or making derogatory remarks about others.
* **Don't Tag Without Permission:** Refrain from posting or tagging photos of your peers without their explicit consent.
* **Avoid Negative Rants:** Do not post aggressive complaints about your professors, college, or workplace online.
* **Don't Ignore Security:** Avoid clicking on suspicious links or joining unverified groups that could compromise your account.
